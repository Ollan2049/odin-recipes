Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a hearty Italian casserole made by layering sheets of pasta with rich meat sauce, béchamel or ricotta, and lots of melted cheese. It’s a comfort food classic that’s perfect for feeding a crowd or enjoying leftovers the next day.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or pork
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Ricotta cheese or béchamel sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Basil and oregano
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the meat with onion and garlic in olive oil until browned.
  2. Add tomato sauce and herbs; let simmer to make a thick meat sauce.
  3. Boil the lasagna noodles until just tender.
  4. In a baking dish, layer sauce, noodles, ricotta/béchamel, and cheese.
  5. Repeat the layers and top with mozzarella and parmesan.
  6. Bake in the oven until bubbling and golden brown on top.
