Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish with a crispy crust, rich tomato sauce, melted cheese, and various toppings. It’s incredibly versatile and can be customized to suit anyone’s taste. A classic comfort food and a favorite for gatherings!

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Basil (optional)
  • Toppings of your choice (pepperoni, mushrooms, olives, etc.)
  1. Preheat your oven to a high temperature (around 220°C / 425°F).
  2. Roll out the dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add shredded mozzarella and your favorite toppings.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and add herbs if desired.
  6. Bake until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
